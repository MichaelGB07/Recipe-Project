Chicken Cutlet Recipe

Description

Cutlet were a typical starter in French cuisine, as a variation of Croquettes with a shape of small rib (c√¥telette in French). The bone was simulated by a piece of fried bread or pasta. The recipe became popular in all Europe due to the influence of French cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2+ Cups of Bread Crumbs
  • Chicken Cutlets
  • Oil
  • Paper Towels

Steps (Homemade/Personal Recipe)

  1. Put oil in a pot and wait till it bubbles
  2. Take out a big bowl and pour the Bread Crumbs
  3. Take out a smaller bowl, crack your eggs into it and whisk them
  4. Wash the Chicken in water, dredge them in the egg wash and then the Bread Crumbs
  5. Fry them in the oil until you get the color you want
  6. Let the finished Cutlets rest of paper towels for 2-5 minutes
